An Open and Scalable Virtual Exchange Platform for Bitcoin.

Bitcoin is at a crossroads. Just a few major currency exchanges or "cambios" serve the majority of the Bitcoin economy. With such a significant majority of Bitcoin transactions operating through just a few cambios, the entire Bitcoin economy is at risk. From over-regulation or with just a few exchanges failing, the Bitcoin economy could collapse as well. We propose the development of a peer-to-peer value exchange platform. This platform will scale the Bitcoin economy, by allowing parties to directly transact with one another in a private and secure fashion. The reliance on a select few cambios can be eliminated. Thus, a much more robust Bitcoin economy can develop and flourish.

FAQ

Why do we need such a platform?
Currently, Bitcoin does not have a virtual currency exchange mechanism. Parties must use individual currency and value exchanges such as Mt. Gox. Unfortunately, the existing exchange model is vulnerable to over regulation or systemic failure. A "virtual" cambio must be developed. Cambix proposes provide system in which privacy of transactions can maintained while insuring that attackers may not subvert the online system.

Isn't OpenCoin's Ripple currency (XRP) going to solve this problem?
First and foremost, OpenCoin's Ripple currency is not truly "open." It is a proprietary system designed to establish Ripples as the primary medium of exchange amongst the parties. Ripples are "pre-mined" property of OpenCoin. As such, the approach neither fits with the philosophy or "fairness" of Bitcoin. Hence, a virtual exchange platform that fits in with the spirit of Bitcoin must be developed. Cambix proposes a system in which Bitcoin, itself, can be used as that medium of exchange.
